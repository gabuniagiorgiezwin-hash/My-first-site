Got a question? The answer is probably here.
Is this site official?
We are an official affiliate/referral partner. Every link leads to a verified official platform (Roblox.com, Minecraft.net, Steam, etc.). We don't sell anything directly — we just connect you to the right store.
Do I pay more than the normal price?
No — you pay exactly the same price as if you went directly to the official store. We receive a commission from the partner platform, not from your wallet.

It means we have a partnership agreement with gaming companies. When you click our link and make a purchase, the company pays us a small percentage as a referral fee. Your money always goes directly to the official company.
What if I have a problem with my purchase?
Since the purchase happens on the official platform, contact their official support team directly. We can also offer general guidance — reach us at info@gameshop.gg.
